About Advanced Gas Products in Orange County

Advanced Gas Products in Huntington Beach, California, is a locally owned and family-operated packaged gas dealer and welding supply. The company was founded in 1989 by Frank Beels. After decades in the industry he saw a market need for an Independent welding supply and gas distributor that would bring back the high level of personal service that customers desire, but cannot get from large corporate suppliers or even big independent stores that subscribe to a or corporate mentality.
